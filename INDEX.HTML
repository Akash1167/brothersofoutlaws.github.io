ABOUT OURSELVES

12th batch of SSPN, the outlaws refers to those who don't follow any rules. Our batch is known for having unity, belief and faith upon each other. We treat each other like families and support then in difficult situations like brothers.

When we join in class 6, no one knows each other and now we became families with the bond that no one can break. We made mistakes, we got punishments, we blamed each other but that was the most memorable part of our life. Parties at late nights, talking unneccessarily at irrelevent topics and plays as naver before. Although, we have grown up but our childhoodness has still in us and that's the best thing we have.
